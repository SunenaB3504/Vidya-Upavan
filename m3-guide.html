📖 Grammar Preview

Key Grammar Concepts

Example Sentence Pattern:
"मी शाळेला जातो." (I go to school.)
Breaking it down: Subject (मी) + Verb (जातो) + Location (शाळेला)

You'll learn how to construct similar sentences with different verbs, subjects, and objects.

💭 Why Grammar Matters

Building Fluency

Understanding grammar helps you construct your own sentences instead of just memorizing phrases. In Phase 3, you'll learn the rules that make Marathi work, allowing you to express your own thoughts and ideas clearly.
