📚 What You'll Learn in Phase 3

🎯 Learning Objectives

Phase 3 covers intermediate to advanced Sanskrit grammar including:

  • Cases (Vibhakti): Nominative, genitive, dative, accusative, ablative, locative, instrumental, vocative
  • Verb Conjugations: Present, past, future tenses with different persons
  • Sentence Patterns: Subject-object-verb relationships and word order flexibility
  • Shlokas & Verses: Introduction to famous Sanskrit couplets and sayings
  • Sandhi Rules: How words connect and phonetic changes at word boundaries
  • Question & Interrogatives: How to form and answer questions in Sanskrit

💭 Grammar Preview

Sample Topics

The Six Cases of Sanskrit:
शरीरम् (body) → शरीरस्य (of the body) → शरीराय (to the body) → शरीरम् (body-object)
Each case shows different relationships and functions in sentences.

Verb Conjugation Example:
गच्छति (he/she goes) | गच्छामः (we go) | गच्छसि (you go)
Verbs change based on who is performing the action.
