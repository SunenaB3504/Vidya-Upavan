📚 About Your Language Adventure

Welcome to Vidya Upavan—a specially designed journey to master Sanskrit and Marathi for students up to Class 5! This program combines the beauty of ancient Sanskrit with the vibrant modern language of Maharashtra, helping you discover the deep connections between both languages.

Through 79 video lessons & interactive guides organized into 4 progressive phases, you'll learn everything from the fundamentals (alphabets and sounds) to storytelling and cultural wisdom. Each phase builds on the previous one, making learning natural and fun!

🎯 Your Learning Journey

🔤 Phase 1: Alphabets
Master Devanagari, vowels, consonants & first words
📚 Phase 2: Vocabulary
Learn body parts, nature, numbers, animals & family
✍️ Phase 3: Grammar
Build sentences, understand verbs & explore shlokas
📖 Phase 4: Stories
Discover Panchatantra, folk tales & cultural heritage

🌉 Three Learning Tracks

🪷 Sanskrit Track — The classical language of wisdom traditions
🌿 Marathi Track — The living language of Maharashtra
🌉 Bridge Track — See how they connect and influence each other!

✨ Tips for Success

  • Watch videos in order—each phase builds on the previous knowledge
  • Mark videos as "Done" to track your progress
  • Repeat lessons until you feel comfortable
  • Pay attention to the Bridge videos to understand connections
  • Most importantly, have fun! 🎉 Learning languages is an adventure!

To be the global 'Garden of Knowledge' for privacy-protected, English-medium Indian language education.

🎯 Our Mission

Providing high-quality Sanskrit, Hindi, and Marathi resources that respect student anonymity—no logins, no tracking, just learning.

Knowledge is a gift that should never cost a child's privacy. We believe in heritage learning open to all, guarded by integrity, and nourished by community.
