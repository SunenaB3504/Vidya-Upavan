⭐ Quick Learning Tips

🎯 How to Remember Vocabulary

  • Group by Theme: Learning words by topic helps build connections
  • Use Examples: Pay attention to how words are used in sentences
  • Practice Writing: Write each word multiple times to remember
  • Speak Aloud: Say words to practice correct pronunciation
  • Create Sentences: Try making your own sentences with new words
  • Relate to English: Notice how some Sanskrit words relate to English words
  • Visualize: Create mental images for each word to remember better

💡 Gender & Number Patterns in Sanskrit

  • Masculine: Often end in -ः or -अ (पुत्रः, गजः)
  • Feminine: Often end in -ी or -आ (माता, नदी)
  • Neuter: Often end in -म् or -अ (फलम्, जलम्)
  • Plural: Words change form to show multiple items
  • Cases: Different word endings show relationships in sentences
